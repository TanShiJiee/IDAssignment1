Introduction:
My name is Shi Jie and I am 17 years old as of 2021. I am a Year 1 student currently pursuing a diploma in Immersive Media at Ngee Ann Polytechnic.

I enjoy Dancing, travelling, and spending time with my family and friends.

Education (2017-2020):
I graduated Hillgrove Secondary School in 2020. It was an enriching experience as I was able to help the community by participating in several Values-In-Action (VIA) projects.

I joined Modern Dance in 2017 and was part of the Exco-Committee from 2019 to 2020. I learnt several skills such as teamwork and communication. In addition, I participated in several school events and SYF 2019.

Work Experience:
I worked as a part-time cosmetic packer at XPO Logistics from 8.30am to 5.30pm from Monday to Friday. It was a valuable experience as I was given the opportunity to learn more about production management and product handling.

VIA Projects:
2017: My class and I organized a mini event in school to publicise about World Water Day by creating posters, mini games with prizes to be won, and managing a booth.
2018: All Secondary 2 students attended "Green @ Hillgrove", where we went to different houses in the area,to gather second-hand items and electronics.
2019: For our Student Initiated Project, my class collaborated with Public Transport Council, helping those in need to carry their items to the bus stop and giving directions.
